Enterprise DOOH Channel · Partner Overview

Admira.tv

For: ASK Corporation · Tosh  ·  June 2026  ·  Confidential

Admira is a European-born DOOH platform that connects AI-created content, physical-space operations, and live screen broadcast — so partners can sell software licenses and managed services on a single, measurable stack.

The problem

Venue networks need scalable content, space-aware operations, and reliable measurable broadcast. Most signage vendors stop at the CMS. Admira closes the loop from creation to revenue.

Bits and Atoms — three layers

01PixerIAAI content studio → Stock (pixeria.com)
02XpaceOSDigital twin: audiences, programmatic, live control (xpaceos.com)
03Admira.tvLive DOOH channel: grid, fleet CMS, players, proof-of-play

Flow: PixerIA creates → XpaceOS operates → Admira.tv goes on air.

Broadcast layer (now)

  • Live channel + fleet CMS
  • Players: macOS, Win, Linux, Android, iOS, tvOS, Echo Show
  • Conditional emission (camera → creative)
  • Grid scheduling + live on-air feed
  • Attention-aware measurement / CPM
