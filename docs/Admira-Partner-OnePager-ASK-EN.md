## The problem we solve

Retail and venue networks need more than a playlist on a screen. They need:

- Content that can be produced at scale (including AI-assisted)
- Operations that understand the physical space (audience, surfaces, campaigns)
- Broadcast that is reliable, segmentable, and measurable in real time

Most signage vendors stop at the CMS. **Admira closes the loop from creation to revenue.**

## Bits and Atoms — three layers, one platform

| Layer | Product | Role |
|:-----:|---------|------|
| **01 · CREATE** | **PixerIA** (pixeria.com) | AI content studio: image, video, audio, campaigns → published to Stock |
| **02 · OPERATE** | **XpaceOS** (xpaceos.com) | Digital twin of the venue: audiences, programmatic sales, live control |
| **03 · BROADCAST** | **Admira.tv** (admira.tv) | The live DOOH channel: grid scheduling, fleet CMS, players, proof-of-play |

**Flow:** PixerIA creates → XpaceOS operates → **Admira.tv goes on air.**

---

## What Admira.tv does (broadcast layer)

- **Live channel** — `admira.tv/canal.html` plays scheduled and conditional content from the PixerIA Stock
- **Fleet CMS** — `admira.tv/cms.html` monitors devices, remote control, segmentation rules, emission calendar
- **Native players** — macOS, Windows, Linux, Android, iOS, iPadOS, tvOS, Amazon Echo Show (kiosk-hardened WebView fleet)
- **Conditional emission** — camera-based audience signals (gender, age, footfall) trigger the right creative in real time
- **Grid programming** — day-part booking per screen; live “on air now” feed from `api.admira.store`
- **Measurement** — impacts, attention (who looks vs who passes), dynamic CPM model; ties to programmatic layer (admira.app)

## Commercial model (for partner discussions)

Clear separation for quoting and contracts:

| Line | What it is | Typical billing |
|------|------------|-----------------|
| **Software license** | Player + channel + CMS access per device or screen | Per screen / year or perpetual + maintenance |
| **Management services** | Campaign setup, scheduling, reporting, creative ops | Retainer or % of media spend |
| **PixerIA (AI content)** | Asset and campaign production | Per asset, per campaign, or creative subscription |
| **Programmatic / data** | Audience targeting, CPM, circuit sales (admira.app) | Media margin or SaaS fee |

*Partners may bundle license + services; Admira supports channel-only or full-stack deals.*
